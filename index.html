Our Story

A legacy of culinary excellence since 1985

A Tradition of Excellence

For nearly four decades, Savoria has been the epitome of fine dining in the heart of the city. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create an extraordinary dining experience that combines exceptional cuisine with impeccable service.

Founded by Chef Alessandro Savori, our restaurant has evolved from a small family-owned establishment to one of the most prestigious dining destinations in the region.

Today, under the leadership of Chef yaaalif, we continue to push the boundaries of culinary innovation while staying true to our roots.
